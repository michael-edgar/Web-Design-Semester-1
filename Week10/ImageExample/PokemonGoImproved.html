Pokémon Go has finally given players a big reason to come back to the game: the introduction of the first new Pokémon since it launched back in July. Pokémon trainers can now catch Ditto, an amorphous blob that can transform into any other Pokemon using its signature move “mimic”. But there’s a catch.

You won’t see Ditto on the world map, the overview screen where players see which Pokémon are around them.
That’ s because it’s hiding in plain sight, disguised as other Pokémon.
In other words, that Ratatta or Pidgey you wouldn’t normally bother with?
If you catch it, it might be a Ditto.
